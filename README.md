## ğŸ“– 5-Minute Tutorial

### Step 1: Choose Your Mode

Click one of the 6 mode buttons in the left sidebar:

- **ğŸ–Œï¸ Paint** - Draw terrain on hexes
- **ğŸª£ Fill** - Fill large connected areas  
- **ğŸ‘† Select** - Click hexes to edit details
- **ğŸ—‘ï¸ Erase** - Remove hexes
- **ğŸ­ Token** - Place character markers
- **ğŸ›¤ï¸ Path** - Draw roads and rivers

### Step 2: Paint Your First Map

1. Click **Paint Mode**
2. Select a terrain from the palette (try "Forest" ğŸŒ²)
3. Click hexes to paint them green
4. Drag to paint multiple hexes quickly
5. Try the **Fill Mode** to paint large areas instantly

### Step 3: Add a Road

1. Click **Path Mode**
2. Click "Road" button
3. Click hexes to draw a path
4. Double-click to finish the road

### Step 4: Place a Token

1. Click **Token Mode**
2. Click "New Token"
3. Name it (e.g., "Party" or "Gandalf")
4. Click a hex to place it
5. Drag the token to move it around

### Step 5: Save Your Work

1. Click **"Export World"** (top-right)
2. Your map downloads as a `.json` file
3. Use **"Import World"** to load it later
